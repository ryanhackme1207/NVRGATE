About Us

NVRGATE is a free platform founded by Ryan in 2022, designed to help students easily access and store notes from different courses. It's a student-friendly hub that makes learning materials more organized, accessible, and shareable—anytime, anywhere.

About NVRGATE

NVRGATE is a free learning platform developed by Ryan, a Bachelor's degree student from the Faculty of Computing and Informatics (FCI), majoring in Computer Science with a focus on Cybersecurity.

  • Founded in 2022 to support students with centralized academic resources.
  • Designed to help students access and store course notes more efficiently.
  • A growing community-driven platform to simplify digital learning experiences.
