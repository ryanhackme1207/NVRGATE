YBhg. Prof. Dato’ Dr. Mazliham Mohd Su’ud

President & CEO of Multimedia University (MMU)

Prof. Dato’ Dr. Mazliham is the 5th President of MMU. With academic roots in France and a PhD in Computer Engineering, he brings extensive expertise in AI, machine learning, and smart technologies. Formerly President of UniKL, he has led international research teams and published 100+ scholarly works. He is also recognized by France and Italy for his contributions to education and international collaboration.

YABhg. Tun Dato’ Seri Zaki Tun Azmi

Chancellor

The 12th Chief Justice of Malaysia, Tun Zaki is known for reforming the judiciary by clearing backlogs and accelerating case resolution. A former magistrate, Federal Judge, and corporate legal expert, he has held key positions in PETRONAS, Malaysia Airports, and more.

Tan Sri Dr. Zakri Abdul Hamid

Pro-Chancellor

Science Adviser to the Prime Minister of Malaysia since 2010, Chairman of MIGHT, BIOTECHCORP, and Board of Advisers at UNU-IIGH. An accomplished academic with degrees from LSU and Michigan State, and a strong background in crop science, biodiversity, and science diplomacy.
